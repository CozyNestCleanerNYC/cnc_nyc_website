OUR COMMERCIAL & HOUSE CLEANING SERVICES IN NYC

Do you wish you had more free time? We can make it happen!

House Cleaning

House Cleaning

We clean your entire home with meticulous attention to detail: dusting ceiling fans and light fixtures, wiping down baseboards and door frames, sanitizing kitchen counters and appliances, scrubbing bathroom tiles and fixtures, vacuuming carpets and upholstery, and mopping all hard floors with specialized cleaners for each surface type.

LEARN MORE

Deep Cleaning

Deep Cleaning

Our deep cleaning service includes everything in a regular cleaning plus: removing scale from shower doors and fixtures, cleaning inside appliances (refrigerator, oven, microwave), washing cabinet fronts, scrubbing grout lines, cleaning window tracks and blinds, sanitizing trash cans, and removing dust from vents, fans, and behind furniture. We use specialized degreasers and sanitizers for maximum results.

LEARN MORE

Commercial Cleaning

Commercial Cleaning

Our commercial cleaning service sanitizes workspaces with hospital-grade disinfectants, focusing on high-touch surfaces like door handles, light switches, and shared equipment. We clean and sanitize restrooms, break rooms, and common areas; vacuum carpets and mop hard floors; empty trash and recycling; clean glass surfaces; and dust all horizontal surfaces including desks, shelving, and window sills.

LEARN MORE

MOVE IN – OUT

MOVE IN – OUT

Our move-in/out cleaning prepares spaces for new occupants by deep cleaning empty rooms: scrubbing inside all cabinets and drawers, cleaning inside and behind appliances, removing dust and cobwebs from ceiling corners, washing all window interiors, cleaning tracks and frames, sanitizing all bathroom surfaces including inside medicine cabinets, and steam cleaning all floors for a fresh start.

LEARN MORE

Airbnb Turnover

Airbnb Turnover

Our Airbnb turnover service ensures your property is guest-ready between bookings. We provide thorough cleaning of all rooms, fresh linens and towels, restocking amenities, sanitizing high-touch surfaces, checking and replacing supplies, and ensuring everything is perfectly arranged for your next guests' arrival.

LEARN MORE

Post-Construction Cleaning

Post-Construction Cleaning

Our post-construction cleaning removes all construction debris, dust, and residue to make your newly built or renovated space move-in ready. We clean all surfaces, remove paint splatters, clean windows inside and out, vacuum and mop all floors, and ensure every corner is spotless and ready for occupancy.
