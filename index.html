Maid Services

Maid Services

Our maids meticulously clean your home using a systematic approach: dusting all surfaces from top to bottom, sanitizing high-touch areas, vacuuming with HEPA filters, and steam-mopping floors. We clean countertops, appliance exteriors, bathroom fixtures, mirrors, and window sills with eco-friendly products that eliminate 99.9% of germs.

House Cleaning

House Cleaning

We clean your entire home with meticulous attention to detail: dusting ceiling fans and light fixtures, wiping down baseboards and door frames, sanitizing kitchen counters and appliances, scrubbing bathroom tiles and fixtures, vacuuming carpets and upholstery, and mopping all hard floors with specialized cleaners for each surface type.

Apartment Cleaning

Apartment Cleaning

Our apartment cleaning tackles every inch of your space: sanitizing kitchen surfaces and appliances, deep-cleaning bathroom tiles and fixtures, dusting all surfaces including hard-to-reach areas, vacuuming under furniture and along baseboards, and treating high-touch areas with disinfectants. We pay special attention to small spaces and storage areas often overlooked.

Deep Cleaning

Deep Cleaning

Our deep cleaning service includes everything in a regular cleaning plus: removing scale from shower doors and fixtures, cleaning inside appliances (refrigerator, oven, microwave), washing cabinet fronts, scrubbing grout lines, cleaning window tracks and blinds, sanitizing trash cans, and removing dust from vents, fans, and behind furniture. We use specialized degreasers and sanitizers for maximum results.

Commercial Cleaning

Commercial Cleaning

Our commercial cleaning service sanitizes workspaces with hospital-grade disinfectants, focusing on high-touch surfaces like door handles, light switches, and shared equipment. We clean and sanitize restrooms, break rooms, and common areas; vacuum carpets and mop hard floors; empty trash and recycling; clean glass surfaces; and dust all horizontal surfaces including desks, shelving, and window sills.
